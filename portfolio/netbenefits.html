netbenefits.html

Overview

Fidelity Investments' NetBenefits web application has provided online account access to retirement savings account investors for many years. I was tasked with leading the redesign of NetBenefits shortly after the milestone of over one million account holders had been reached.

Project Goals

Call center representative were fielding many questions that were the result of customers not being able to find the information they needed, even though it was available online. Additionally, there were still a number of straightforward financial transactions that required the assistance of a call center rep.

Improve findability by updating site navigation scheme; handle financial transactions in a modal fashion.

User Research

What are the categories of information that users are seeking, and what are the various actions that they want to perform. What differentiates task flows that are purely informational vs. those that comprise a sequence of steps within a financial transaction.

Analysis

Users prefer ubiquitous navigation schemes, but will tolerate having navigation options removed when necessary for the integrity of performing a financial transaction such as exchanging shares among mutual funds.

Design

Branding/color palette determined by graphic design agency; all about information architecture, task flows and ensuring the integrity of financial transactions.

Validation

User testing in state-of-the-art usability lab; two rounds of testing with interactive prototypes to validate design
