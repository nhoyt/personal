Accessibility Bookmarklets

The Accessibility Bookmarklets make visible various accessibility features on a web page that are otherwise hidden, including heading levels, landmarks, form control/label relationships, list markup and image alternative text.

In collaboration with members of the local accessibility community, I designed and developed a set of five bookmarklets, which are freely available and easily installable in any browser.

The Accessibility Bookmarklets have become popular among campus web designers and developers. They provide a lightweight method of making an initial assessment of whether a web page meets the accessibility requirements within a given category of compliance.
