Allstate Insurance: Call Center Resources App

The Call Center Resources App for Allstate Insurance involved designing an application that would provide a unified source of information for call center representatives to reference in answering questions for policy holders.

This project depended heavily on creating an information architecture that aligned with the mental models that call center reps had regarding the categories of information they needed to access.

To learn more about these mental models, I conducted a number of task modeling sessions, in which a group of call center representatives were asked about their day-to-day activities, and the types of information they needed to reference, as well as the best labels for these categories.

Through iterative refinement of the labels for various categories of information, where group consensus was sought until there was agreement that each label provided the correct “information scent”, a successful IA was created that significantly increased the efficiency of finding the appropriate information needed for the task at hand.
